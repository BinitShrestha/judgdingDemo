Judging Form



Quality – the product is consistently good quality and appealing
Innovation - the product is innovative and adds something new to the marketplace
Demonstration – the functionality of the product is apparent
Price – the price-point is appropriate for the product


Product Description – the product is described clearly, including the problem it solves and the value it adds
Intro & Closing – pitch begins with a friendly, engaging opening and ends with a clear “ask” or closing
Presentation Skills – good physical composure (posture, pacing, hand gestures), eye contact and voice projection
Passion – the sales pitch is enthusiastic, persuasive and passionate


Timely - Customer was greeted in a timely, friendly, professional manner
Responsive – team responded to questions and concerns quickly, thoroughly and professionally
Transaction – transaction process was smooth and professional
Collaboration – all team members contributed to providing strong customer service


Organization – display was organized and neatly put together
Informative – display contained all relevant information about the business (price, contact info, etc.)
Attractive – display was visually appealing and attracted customers and sales
